Tips to reduce food waste at different places

Tips to reduce food waste at home

  • Use eggshells for plants
  • Use expired bread to feed the birds
  • Use spoiled fruit for plants
  • Use overripe bananas to bake a cake
  • Use spoiled vegetables for composting
  • Use coffee beans to make skin care
  • Use spoiled milk to make cheese
  • Use spoiled milk to make yogurt

Tips to reduce food waste at food manufacturing

Some of the food waste and food loss are also coming from food manufacturing .
However, there are a lot of ways to reduce them too. Some are :

  • Analytics and forecasting methods,
  • Empowering staff
  • Application of digital learning practice

Empowering staff means the staffs can sort out on how to prevent food loss by food mangement during food manufacturing by measuring the rate of food loss.

Tips to reduce food waste at farm

Adopt automated systems to:

  • Enable 24/7 operations
  • Ensure precise planting, harvesting, and sorting
  • Meet SKU specifications set by buyers
  • Reduce reliance on manual labor

Implement precision agriculture technologies such as:

  • Soil monitoring
  • Crop health analysis
  • Predictive analytics (e.g., weather forecasting, yield predictions)
  • To optimize harvests and minimize losses

Our History

Our organization, which is a zero-waste system, began with a simple journey. First, all our team members sought out the local issues happening all over the world. Soon, we found out that food waste is a global issue that so many people are facing. We decided to create a sustainable website, which is about food waste. Whoever visits our website, we ensure that everyone can handle the food waste effectively to lead a sustainable life for everyone. Moreover, we create our own organization logo and put interactive quizzes and games as well.
